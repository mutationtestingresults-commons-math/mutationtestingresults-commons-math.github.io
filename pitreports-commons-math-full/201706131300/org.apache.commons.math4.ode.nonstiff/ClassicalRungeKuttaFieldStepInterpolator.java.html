36

37 
 *                                     + (   -3 &theta; + 4 &theta;<sup>2</sup>) y'<sub>4</sub>
38 
 *                                    ]
39

40

41

42

43

44

45 
 *                                        ]
46 
 *   </li>
47 
 * </ul>
48 
 *
49 
 * where &theta; belongs to [0 ; 1] and where y'<sub>1</sub> to y'<sub>4</sub> are the four
50

51
