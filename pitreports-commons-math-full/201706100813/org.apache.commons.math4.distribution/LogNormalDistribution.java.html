/**

31 
 *
32 
 * <p>
33 
 * <strong>Parameters:</strong>
34 
 * {@code X} is log-normally distributed if its natural logarithm {@code log(X)}
35 
 * is normally distributed. The probability distribution function of {@code X}
36 
 * is given by (for {@code x > 0})
37

38 
 * <p>
39
